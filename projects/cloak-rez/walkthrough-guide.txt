# Cloak of Darkness (Rez) — Walkthrough Guide
# A choice-based adaptation of the standard IF benchmark.
# Two paths: win (2/2) or lose (1/2). Choices listed by button label.

## Victory -- Best Ending (2/2)
# Hang your cloak before entering the bar. Read the undisturbed message.
> Enter the foyer
> Go west to the cloakroom
> Hang your cloak on the hook
> Return to the foyer
> Go south to the bar
> Read the message

## Defeat -- Bad Ending (1/2)
# Enter the bar while still wearing the cloak. Stumble in the dark.
> Enter the foyer
> Go south to the bar
> Fumble around in the dark
> Fumble around in the dark
> Go back north to the foyer
> Go west to the cloakroom
> Hang your cloak on the hook
> Return to the foyer
> Go south to the bar
> Read the message
