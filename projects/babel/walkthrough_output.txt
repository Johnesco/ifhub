



Babel Fish Puzzle Permutation 2013 - An Homage To Adams and Meretzky
An Interactive Fiction by Taupelink
Release 1 / Serial number 260312 / Inform 7 v10.1.2 / D

Vogon Hold
A squalid room. Along one wall is a dispenser. A large fan, built in to the floor, intermittently blasts a jet of air up into the room. You have items in your inventory. An intercom repeats an unintelligible three-word phrase.

>It has a button.

>A Babel fish shoots out of the dispenser. It arcs downward.
It falls into a drain.

>You are carrying:
  a gown
  a towel
  a satchel
  a loose pile of mail

>You put the towel on the drain.

>A Babel fish shoots out of the dispenser. It arcs downward.
The Babel fish lands on the towel. A floorborne robot appears, grabs the fish, and whizzes toward a panel in the wall.
The floor robot zips through the panel.

>You put the satchel on the panel.

>A Babel fish shoots out of the dispenser. It arcs downward.
The Babel fish lands on the towel. A floorborne robot appears, grabs the fish, and whizzes toward a panel in the wall.
The floor robot plows into the satchel, sending the Babel fish flying. It continues flying.
It sails through a small hole in the wall, just under a metal hook.

>You put the gown on the metal hook.

>A Babel fish shoots out of the dispenser. It arcs downward.
The Babel fish lands on the towel. A floorborne robot appears, grabs the fish, and whizzes toward a panel in the wall.
The floor robot plows into the satchel, sending the Babel fish flying. It continues flying.
The Babel fish bounces off of the gown. It is lifted toward the ceiling by a sudden gust from the fan. An airborne robot flies into the room.
The air robot catches the Babel fish (which is all the flying junk it can find) and exits.

>You put the loose pile of mail on the large fan.

>A Babel fish shoots out of the dispenser. It arcs downward.
The Babel fish lands on the towel. A floorborne robot appears, grabs the fish, and whizzes toward a panel in the wall.
The floor robot plows into the satchel, sending the Babel fish flying. It continues flying.
The Babel fish bounces off of the gown. It is lifted into a cloud of junk mail by a sudden gust from the fan. An airborne robot flies into the room.
The air robot starts collecting the plume of mail. The fish is ignored.
The Babel fish lands in your ear. You can suddenly understand the announcement on the intercom. It says:


    *** You have won ***




Would you like to RESTART, RESTORE a saved game, QUIT or UNDO the last command?
> 
<end of input>
