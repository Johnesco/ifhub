## Getting Started -- Entrance Hall

> take brass key

Pick up the brass key from the hall floor. You'll need it later.

## The Journal Clue -- Library

> n

Head north to the library.

> open journal

Read the dusty journal on the reading desk. It hints: "The treasure is hidden where water meets stone." (+2 points)

## The Garden Coin -- Garden

> s

Return south to the Entrance Hall.

> s

Continue south into the garden.

> take rusty coin

Grab the old coin from the garden. (+2 points)

## Where Water Meets Stone -- Pond

> s

Follow the path south to the pond.

> push flat stones

Remember the journal's clue? Push the loose stones at the water's edge to reveal a golden ring. (+3 points)

> take golden ring

Pick up the ring.

## Return to the Library -- Backtracking

> n

Go north to the garden.

> n

Continue north to the Entrance Hall.

> n

North again to the library.

## The Locket -- Study

> up

Climb the narrow staircase to the study.

> unlock cabinet with brass key

Use the brass key you picked up at the start.

> open cabinet

Open the cabinet to reveal the silver locket.

> take silver locket

Take the locket to complete your adventure. (+3 points, victory!)
