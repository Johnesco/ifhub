## Stage 1 — The Spectacles

# You wake in the Receiving Room. Pick up the spectacles
# and put them on — they reveal things invisible to the naked eye.

> take spectacles
> wear spectacles

# Go east to the Corridor. With spectacles, you notice a
# trapdoor outlined in the floor tiles.

> e

# Descend through the trapdoor to the Stairwell.
# (Impossible without spectacles — the floor looks solid.)

> down

# Pick up the pipe wrench from the bottom step.

> take wrench

# Go south to the Laboratory. With spectacles, you spot
# a glass case on the far wall containing a brass key.

> s

## Stage 2 — The Fungus

# Smash the glass case with the wrench to get the brass key.

> break glass case

# Go east to Cold Storage.

> e

# Unlock the metal cabinet (stamped with a snowflake) using
# the brass key. It swings open to reveal a grey fungus
# labelled PERCEPTUAL AGENT — STAGE 2 THERAPY.

> unlock cabinet with key

# Take the fungus and eat it. The world transforms — chairs
# become crouching things, pipes become arteries. A passage
# south from the Laboratory opens where solid floor used to be.

> take fungus
> eat fungus

# Return to the Laboratory.

> w

# Descend south into the Cistern. Chemical spray from cracked
# pipes distorts your perception — text becomes corrupted.

> s

## Stage 3 — The Source

# The sign says LEFT drains, RIGHT floods. Turn the valve left
# to drain the cistern and reveal the passage below.
# (WARNING: turning right is instant death.)

> turn valve left

# Descend through the drainage grate to The Source.

> down

# Touch the glowing stone basin to end the game.

> touch basin
